API Project: URL Shortener Microservice

Hello everyone. In this project you can enter a url in the form below and by sumbitting it, you will receive its shorter version in a json format.
Next, if you enter in the url of your browser /api/shorturl/theShortUrl, where theShortUrl will be equal to the number you received when you submited your original link, you will be redirected to the original website you submited.

!Important Note! You don't need to enter 'http://' or 'https://' in your url otherwise it will throw you an error. Just www.yoururl......
